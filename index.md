Ongoing advances in artificial intelligence are necessitating that mathematicians re-imagine workflows, objective measurement, and their relationship with the discipline.
We are offering an intensive five-day workshop to rapidly introduce researchers to the foundations of AI and computation.
The workshop will introduce practical AI tools and demonstrate how they can be integrated into mathematical research.

## When and where
The workshop will run **Wednesday, October 28th to Sunday, November 1st** at *UC-Santa Cruz*.

## Funding
We will be able to provide travel and lodging support for participants. Applications for funding will open soon.
